The Seasons of the year

All Year Around

The Seasons of the year can differ from very cold to super hot; from soaking wet or even harshly dry.In different places around the world it can be the same climate all year. But no matter what season it is, you will be sure to enjoy life!

The year is made up of four seasons!
A perpetual quad-cycle if you will!
